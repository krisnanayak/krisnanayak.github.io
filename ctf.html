VeniVidiVici

  • CTF Introduction
  Duration: 18 hrs     Team Size: 3

In this jeopardy-styled CTF, you have to put your hacking skills to practice and capture various flags hidden throughout the websites/IPs given to you.

Here are some of the types of skills that wil be tested, given so that you'll put up a good fight !

  • Pwning
  • Cryptography
  • Web

Each flag gives a certain amount of points to the team that captures it (points vary flag to flag, depending on how hard the exploit is). Top 3 will siply be the teams with the highest points.

Participants are expected to get their own laptops with apt software stack.
